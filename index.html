About This Portfolio ▼

This portfolio is for Project 2 in CPSC 581. Our objective was to create a sensor-based phone unlocking system that is unique in terms of both usefulness and originality. Each team member first contributed original ideas. In order to generate ideas and experiment with notions influenced by personal experiences and real-world situations, each of us began with ten preliminary sketches. During the brainstorming phase, each member contributed unique insights. One member suggested a shake-to-unlock feature that triggered an animation, while another proposed a voice-activated unlocking method using ambient sound detection. Another teammate explored gestures based on gripping patterns. Our challenge was to narrow down the options to a single, feasible design. After some feedback from peers, we started eliminating ideas which could be time consuming. We decided on a gesture-based unlocking mechanism that captures both user interaction and creativity (it was inspired from Bobba tea example shown in class). Our final approach integrates elements from each initial idea, creating a balance between uniqueness and simplicity to ensure it can be effectively prototyped within our timeline
