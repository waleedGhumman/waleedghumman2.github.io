Final Storyboard

Amongst the rough sketches, I chose Sketch 7 as my final project because it closely resembles my vision for the user interface. I get blood in my nose, brittle hair and dry skin because Calgary has dry weather. So I have to turn on humidifer everytime. Often I get lazy and dont turn it on which causes me problems. So I thought it would be so cool to have smart humidifer

Storyboard 1: I am lazy I come to my room and head to bed, Humidifer turns on based on my settings.

Storyboard 2: I leave the room, smart humidifer turns off

Storyboard 3: someone else enters, and the humidifer turns on based off their settings

Storyboard 4: A mother is allerigic to humidifier but not her baby, so all the mist goes straight to baby
